2. High E_dens (>35 kWh/m3) requires high dT_TES_rel_sp (>0.6)
   → Larger TES temperature span needed for high energy density
3. Winter working points dominate due to low T_cs=5°C
4. SRVCHP+SRORC dominates because regenerators reduce irreversibilities in both cycles
